Football

Minnesota Vikings



My favorite football team in the NFL are the Minnesota Vikings. I have been a huge fan since a young child and through good and bad, I will always be cheering for them. I just want them to win a Super Bowl!! SKOL!

Baseball

Minnesota Twins

My favorite baseball team in the MLB are the Minnesota Twins. It has been great that they have won two World Series titles since I was born, I was very young though. It is nice to see them succeeding again and I am hoping for more success in the future!

Basketball

Minnesota Timberwolves

My favorite team in the NBA are the Minnesota Timberwolves. It is very hard being such a big fan because they have not been a successful team in the league. We have some hope with some young stars on the team this year and I can not wait until they are back in the playoffs and winning games!

Hockey

Minnesota Wild

My favorite team in the NHL are the Minnesota Wild. They are a very consistant team and always making the playoffs. They just tend to blow it in the playoffs and get knocked out before the Stanley Cup. Maybe luck will go there way soon and bring home a title!
